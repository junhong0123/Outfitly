# Outfitly Store Policies

## Shipping
Outfitly offers standard delivery within 3 to 5 business days after an order is processed. Free standard shipping is available for orders over $100. Delivery time can vary during public holidays or peak shopping periods.

## Returns
Customers can request a return within 30 days of purchase. Returned items should be unused, unworn, and in their original condition. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method after the returned items are inspected.

## Exchanges
Size or color exchanges are allowed when stock is available. If the requested size or color is unavailable, customers can choose a refund or select another product.

## Payment
Outfitly supports card payment during checkout. For demo and academic testing, payment details are used only to simulate checkout flow and should not be treated as production payment processing.

## Stock And Sizing
Product availability depends on the current stock quantity in Outfitly's database. If a size is not listed for a product, it should be treated as unavailable.

## AI Assistant Scope
The AI assistant can help with product discovery, product information, size availability, shipping, returns, and payment policy. It cannot access private account details or personal order history in the current version.
